Graphic Designer

I'm currently working as a designer at GHD, a NYC based studio with clients including American Express, Google and Saks Fifth Avenue. I have a bachelors of Visual Communications and Design from UniverCidade in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
In 2010, I moved to New York City to continue my typography studies at the School of Visual Arts. I'm now working towards a certification in front-end web development at General Assembly NY. Prior to joining GHD, I also worked at the Kirshenbaum Bond Senecal + Partners New York office and the Osklen Group Headquarters in Rio de Janeiro. I was born and raised in Brazil, I speak portuguese, spanish and English.
